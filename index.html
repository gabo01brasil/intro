somewhere between the real and the noise,

there are hidden things waiting to be found.

they pulse at low frequencies,

recorded on the underside of things —

carrying filters the world lost long ago.

maybe you're the kind of person

who finds what wasn't meant to be found.

find the lost QR codes,

scan them and help the lost filters come back to life.
